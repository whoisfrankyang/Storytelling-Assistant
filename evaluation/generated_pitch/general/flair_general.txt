Enter 'FLAIR', a revolutionary robot-assisted feeding system designed to handle a wide variety of meals and cater to individual tastes. Unlike existing systems, FLAIR uses advanced technology to plan and execute efficient meal sequences based on user preferences. In real-world tests, FLAIR efficiently served food to diverse individuals, proving its adaptability across different institutions and robots. This innovation promises to restore the joy of eating, impacting not just the individual but also their caregivers, ultimately redefining the dining experience for those with mobility limitations."
